Company Placement Process
Description: Ramco Systems Placement Process
The selection process typically consists of:
Round 1: Online Aptitude Test
Assessing technical skills, problem-solving abilities, and logical reasoning.
Duration: 60-90 minutes
Round 2: Technical Interview
Evaluating programming skills, coding expertise, and technical knowledge.
Focus on Java, Python, C++, Data Structures, and Algorithms.
Round 3: HR Interview
Assessing communication skills, personality, and cultural fit. Discussion on career goals, motivation, and job expectations.
Additional Rounds (if required) may involve group discussions or further technical interviews.
